My Next Days - Alternative Design

This is the paper prototype for an alternative design of the My Next Days prototype.
This design differs in the following aspects:
  1. Additional date picker
    Reason: For users who want to use the date picker (sometimes its easier to choose a date if you see the calendar representration in front of you)
  2. Additional search field, instead of using same field for adding an enttry and searching for entries
    Reason: A dedicated serach field may be known from other applications, as Firefox for example)
The animation shows:
  1. Add entry (making use of the deta picker)
  2. Search (making use of the extra input field)
  3. Remove (same as in original design)
